Our Famous Dish

Biryani is a fragrant rice dish made with basmati rice and a blend of aromatic spices.
It originated in the Indian subcontinent and is now popular in many countries across South Asia and the Middle East.
It is typically served with raita (yogurt sauce), boiled eggs, and sometimes salad or pickle for added flavor.
